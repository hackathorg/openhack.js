Features

Unifying the hackathon experience

We've developed hackath.org as a base for all types of Hackathon attendee, whether you are a sponsor, a host or an attendee. We offer all the tools you need to discover, design and attend events across the country.

One click Hackathon hosting

Primarily, hackath.org allows users to deploy a fully customisable hackathon framework in one click, allowing anyone to design and create their own Hackathon event. Once deployed, organisers can tailor the event to their own needs, using our unique modular framework design.

Check out our demo!

Unique Hackathon profiling

Once you've set up your hackath.org account, we can customise the hackathon experience for your individual needs. This ranges from team builders at individual hackathon events, to a leaderboard of the best and brightest hackers on our site!

Open-Source development

Our entire development is Open-Source! We know hackers love to tinker, and for advanced users, the entire development stack is available to work with! You can even host the service as a stand-alone and find your own hosting solutions!

Check it out on GitHub!
